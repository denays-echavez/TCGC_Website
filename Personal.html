Faith

Faith gives meaning and direction to my life. It helps me stay hopeful during challenges and reminds me to do good, be kind, and trust in God in everything that I do.

“Faith is a living, daring confidence in God’s grace, so sure and certain that a man could stake his life on it a thousand times.” — Martin Luther King Jr.

Integrity

Integrity means doing what is right even when no one is watching. It encourages me to be honest, respectful, and responsible in my actions.

“The ultimate measure of a man is not where he stands in moments of comfort and convenience, but where he stands at times of challenge and controversy.” — Martin Luther King Jr.

Responsibility in Digital Citizenship

Responsible digital citizenship means using technology ethically, respecting others online, and thinking before sharing or posting anything.

“Always think before you click, post, or share, because every action online has an impact.”

My Inspirational Message

Live with faith in your heart, integrity in your character, and responsibility in your actions.
